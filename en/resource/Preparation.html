1.1. Preparation for Integration

Tuya Smart iOS Device Control Biz Bundle relies on the Tuya Smart Home iOS SDK. Before starting to develop with the SDK, you need to register a developer account, create a product, etc. on the Tuya Smart Development Platform, and obtain a key to activate the SDK, please refer to Tuya Smart Home iOS SDK Preparation work.
